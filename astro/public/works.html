Method: OSU
SZU
Logo
Orthogonalized State Update (OSU)
To prevent rank collapse, we constrain the memory evolution on the Stiefel Manifold:
$$\mathcal{V}_{C_{v},C_{k}}=\{S\in\mathbb{R}^{C_{v}\times C_{k}}:S^{\top}S=I_{C_{k}}\}$$
$$S_{t}=\mathrm{Proj}_{\mathcal{V}}(S_{t}^{\mathrm{Euc}})$$
1.
High-order Newton-Schulz Iteration: Efficient projection without expensive SVD computation.
2.
Geometric Stability: Preserves fine-grained valvular motion across temporal transitions.

Method: APFE
SZU
Logo
Anatomical Prior-aware Feature Enhancement
A physics-driven process explicitly separates anatomical structures from speckle noise:
1.
Acoustic Bias Estimation
$$M_{t}=AvgPool_{K\times K}(X_{t})$$
2.
Polarity-aware Decoupling
$$X_{t}^{+}=ReLU(X_{t}-M_{t}), \quad X_{t}^{-}=ReLU(M_{t}-X_{t})$$
3.
$X_{t}^{+}$ isolates high-frequency edges (myocardium), while $X_{t}^{-}$ captures low-response regions (blood pools).
